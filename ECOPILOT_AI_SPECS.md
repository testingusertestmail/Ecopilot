# EcoPilot AI — Technical Specifications & Architecture Guide

EcoPilot AI (**Measure. Understand. Reduce.**) is a modular full-stack application built using React 19, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Express, and Google's `@google/genai` model ecosystem. 

This guide details the custom scientific algorithms, gamification loops, interactive structures, and real-time AI simulators built to help users reduce their carbon footprints.

---

## 🚀 Key Architectural Paradigms

1. **Full-Stack Isolation**: All Gemini AI core queries are proxied and calculated through Express-side server handlers (`/api/*`). Secure API keys are stored exclusively in background environment contexts and never exposed to the client.
2. **Interactive Front-End State**: State transitions utilize `AnimatePresence` layout animations via `motion/react` to provide micro-interaction tactile feedback.

### 🧠 AI Decision Intelligence Pipeline

EcoPilot AI employs a structured, sequential AI decision intelligence loop that flows dynamically from initial evaluation to real-world behavioral changes:

```text
User Carbon Assessment (Input Survey & Daily Logs)
              ↓
Sector Emission Breakdown (Detailed Category Metrics)
              ↓
AI Reduction Coach (Individualized Pathway Scoping)
              ↓
What-If Scenario Simulator (Interactive Lifestyle Controls)
              ↓
Goal Selection (Timelines and Percent Reduction Triggers)
              ↓
AI Roadmap Generator (Month-by-Month Transition Planning)
              ↓
Dynamic Challenge Generator (Gamified Campaign Quests)
              ↓
Progress Tracking (Verification Metrics and Streak Rewards)
              ↓
Carbon Health Improvement (Sustained Footprint Reductions)
```

By connecting these nodes, EcoPilot AI turns static numbers into a guided ecological development plan.

## 🛠️ Developed Structural Features

### 1. 🎛️ AI Carbon Reduction Coach
* **Action**: Located at `/api/reduction-coach`, it calculates customized pathways using the user's exact sector emissions.
* **Metadata Outputs**:
  * **Ease of Implementation**: Metric scoring (e.g., Easy, Moderate, Hard).
  * **Cost-Effectiveness**: Dynamic tier (e.g., Low Cost, Invest to Save, Zero Cost).
  * **Difficulty Match**: Standardized grading.
  * **Environmental Equivalency**: Real-world contextual equivalents.
  * **Annual Saving**: Expressive reduction in kilograms of CO₂e.

### 2. 🎚️ What-If Scenario Simulator
* **Action**: Fully client-side reactive simulation widget allowing users to toggle multi-sector lifestyle adjustments (Vegetarian Diet, Solar Panel array installation, Commuting alternatives, Thrift consumption, Telecommuting cycles).
* **Math Formulas**:
  $$\text{Savings} = C_{\text{transit}} + (D_{\text{diet}} \times 0.45) + (E_{\text{energy}} \times 0.70) + C_{\text{compost}} + (C_{\text{consumption}} \times 0.35) + C_{\text{telecommute}}$$
* **Interactive Values**: Dynamically displays **Before Signature**, **After Signature**, and **Cumulative Saving Tons** in real-time.

### 3. 🎯 Carbon Footprint Health Score & Letter Grade
* **Metric System**: Translates absolute kilograms into an ecological wellness scale ranging from $0 \text{ to } 100$:
  $$\text{Score}_{\text{raw}} = 100 - \max\left(0, \min\left(85, \frac{\text{Emissions}_{\text{gross}} - 2000}{130}\right)\right)$$
* **Calibration**: Baseline thresholds are calibrated against commonly referenced sustainable per-capita annual emission targets (e.g., IPCC 2.0t per capita limit) and adjusted using reduction offsets.
* **Bonus Offsets**: Adds up to $+15$ bonus points based on user active offsets logged.
* **Grades**: Evaluated as **A+** (Zero Carbon Hero), **A**, **B+**, **B**, **C+**, **C**, **D**, and **F** backed by direct tactical warnings.

### 4. 📊 Carbon Twin (Digital Twin)
* Provides side-by-side comparative visualizations (**Current You** vs. **Eco You**), projecting standard optimizations to motivate immediate physical steps.
* **Eco Twin Projection Logic**: "Eco You" is dynamically generated by applying the highest-impact recommendations identified by the AI Reduction Coach and What-If Simulator. Projected carbon footprint is calculated as:
  $$\text{Projected CO}_2 = \text{Current CO}_2 - \text{Recommended Savings}$$
  This immediately allows users to visualize their achievable environmental profile.

### 4.5. 📊 Community Benchmark Engine
* **Regional Comparison**: Compares the user's specific emissions and targeted reductions against Regional Averages, Sustainable Target (IPCC 2.0t limit), and User Target Goals directly inside the reduction dashboard to answer, "How am I doing?"

### 5. 🎯 Goal-Based Reduction Targets
* **Custom Control**: Slider inputs to define desired target percentages (5% to 50%) and timeline periods (3 to 24 Months).
* **Progress Bar**: Accumulates and charts all verified carbon-mitigating daily logged actions and project offsets against the total goal threshold.

### 6. 🌲 Real-Life Equivalency Engine
* Translates standard abstract carbon mass into real physical equivalents utilizing US Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) coefficients:
  * **Trees Planted**: $\text{CO}_2 \text{ kg} / 22$ (Absorption rate of mature pine)
  * **Gasoline Liters Saved**: $\text{CO}_2 \text{ kg} / 2.3$ (Fuel chemical density)
  * **Smartphone Charges**: $\text{CO}_2 \text{ kg} \times 120$ (Average battery power equivalents)

### 7. ⚡ Smart Home / Electricity Trend Board
* **Visual Grid**: Multi-month electricity usage visualizer bar chart.
* **AI Diagnostics**: Powered by `/api/smart-home-analysis` checking month-on-month load trends, warning users about standby phantom power draw and thermal comfort cooling profiles.

### 8. 📋 AI Challenge Generator
* Uses Gemini models at `/api/challenge-generator` to analyze the highest carbon-contributing sectors and dynamically output customized physical campaign quests. Players can accept these quests directly into their gamified dashboard.

### 9. 💬 AI Sustainability Advisor (Formerly EcoPilot AI Chatbot)
* **Action**: Provides climate-focused advisory assistance, reduction planning, footprint interpretation, sustainability education, and personalized action guidance. Powered through `/api/chat`.
* Multi-Turn chat capabilities utilizing dynamic conversation history rendering. Includes full fallback guides.

### 10. 🗺️ Interactive Month-by-Month AI Carbon Reduction Roadmap
* **Action**: Located at `/api/roadmap-generator`, it analyzes target reduction percentages (5% to 50%) and goal timelines (3 to 12 months) to synthesize a modular monthly step-by-step carbon-saving pathway.
* **Timeline Selector**: Features a horizontal month selector menu, allowing users to select individual month nodes to view specific milestone progress descriptions, local action targets, and estimated carbon drops in real-time.

### 11. 🏆 Eco Streak Engine
* **Engagement Loop**: Tracks consecutive days of carbon-saving actions with custom visual micro-animations, rewarding daily commitments with bonus XP, milestone badges, and streak multipliers to build lasting conservation habits.

## 📐 Dashboard Information Hierarchy

To maximize readability and visual storytelling, our dashboard organizes and centers user focus on high-impact insights in sequential top-down order:
1. **Carbon Health Score**: Real-time evaluation grade (e.g., B+) and absolute tonnes metric.
2. **Annual Footprint**: Instant visual breakdown of transport, dietary, and utility sectors.
3. **Carbon Twin Comparator**: Side-by-side visualization showing current lifestyle vs. simulated optimal ("Eco You") projection.
4. **Community Benchmark Engine**: High-fidelity indicator comparing footprint against global records and sustainable IPCC thresholds.
5. **What-If Scenario Simulator**: Toggles to test compound effects of switching to a green lifestyle.
6. **AI Reduction Coach**: Micro-implementations categorized by ease, implementation cost, and equivalency.
7. **Climate Goal Journey**: Target slider tracking actual metrics against timelines.
8. **AI Carbon Reduction Roadmap**: Interactive node timeline mapping step-by-step monthly targets.
9. **Smart Challenges**: Accepted carbon quests with accepted progression states.
10. **AI Sustainability Advisor**: Immediate conversational terminal with intelligent climate context.
11. **Smart Home Analytics**: Historical electricity bar-charts with automated load diagnostic feedback.

EcoPilot AI is designed to directly map to and fulfill the four core requirements of the carbon reduction challenge:

| Challenge Pillar | EcoPilot AI Module & Scientific Proof-points |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Measure** | Sector surveys, offset trackers, and daily log registries calculating emissions using IPCC/EPA scientific parameters. |
| **Understand** | Multi-sector footprint breakdown, month-on-month Smart Home grids, and the Community Benchmark Engine comparative tracker. |
| **Reduce** | Step-by-step month roadmaps, interactive What-If scenario simulation, and customized AI reduction suggestions. |
| **Track (Simple Actions)** | Gamified physical quests, daily streak multiplier rewards, and the Climate Goal milestone tracking bar. |
